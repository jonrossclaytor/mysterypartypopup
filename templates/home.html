What We Do

Write, plan, and host customized events and parties for groups of 25-40 of your friends. We have pre-made parties that can be catered to your group’s unique personalities, or we can create a new party that is molded to your specific interests! Our parties are meant to be interactive, unique, and customized to fit the needs of your group. We touch on inside jokes and personalities, and will be sure to make this a night you’ll never forget.

What You Do

Assist us in writing, planning, and throwing an interactive party for 25-40 of your friends, colleagues, teammates, or anyone else you may want this experience with! Your help is appreciated throughout our creation process, as well as the party / performance. Insider information of your group is key for our process, by making the night more comfortable and hilarious for those in attendance.
