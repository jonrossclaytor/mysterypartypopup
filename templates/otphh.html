The Old Town Prep

Homecoming Horror


Synopsis


It’s the night of the #OldTownPrepHomecomingJamboree (#OTPHJ), on the eve of the homecoming football game against the Mostly Minnesotans, and the school is in utter turmoil. Drug and alcohol use, along with rampant sexual acts (in the halls, rooms, and closets of the school) are forcing The Staff to reach a boiling point. The Football Team is riding high, since winning their last 2 games after star Quarterback, Shane Moxi, went down with a career ending injury. His now former girlfriend, Kelli, is done with him and is moving on to other boys in the school, but just how many? That is a hot topic of conversation in the halls and amongst her crew, The Cheerleaders, these days. While not all are involved in the school spirit surrounding the OTPHJ as say, The Smart Kids (an individualistic squad- filled with brilliance and #ponyspirit), or even The Basketball Team (a team in complete disarray- with a unique cast of characters) are- ALL students present at the OTPHJ are involved in the #sexdrugsandponychasing rumors swirling around the night. The Mean Girls have a secret they’ve been keeping from the school that they’ll do ANYTHING to keep under wraps, while The Scene Kids revel in others misfortunes- spending the night being way too cool for everyone. The Foreign Exchange Students seem mostly lost at times in the rumors and happenings of the night, but is this a sly maneuver to feed a secret desire for blood? The night will unfold many secrets that are cultivated from seemingly silly rumors, but no matter what, the highlight of the night will surely be the performance of a new single by the soon-to- be famous, Brandon Norris, of The BN4. Pay attention to the rumors and clues that will be laid out for you during this night of terror. It’s a school of deceit, lies, and mischief of course, so be sure to stay true to your character no matter what HORROR takes place.
